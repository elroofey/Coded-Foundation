About Us

At Coded Foundation for Technology Development, our founding team comprises dedicated technologists, educators, and social activists who believe in the transformative power of technology.

Rufai Bello

Founder & CEO

Rufai Bello is a seasoned Data Scientist and AI specialist with expertise in Python programming and software development. ... He holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Bayero University, Kano, and an M.Sc. in the same field from Kaduna State University. As a professional member of Data Science Nigeria and a Fellow of the ClickonKaduna Data Science Fellowship, Rufai has led numerous impactful projects in data science, artificial intelligence, and software development. Passionate about technology advocacy, he is committed to empowering underrepresented groups, bridging the tech skills gap, and promoting opportunities for women in technology.

Dr. Mustapha Lawal

Co-Founder & CTO

Dr. Mustapha is a tech innovator and advocate for women in technology. ... Cultivating a journey from the foundational steps of my education at Hauwa Memorial Primary School, I have evolved into a seasoned professional in the realm of Computer Science. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, from Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University, I have honed my expertise through years of dedicated academic pursuit. My academic odyssey commenced with a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Bayero University, Kano, where I graduated with Second Class Honours (Upper Division). Subsequently, I furthered my studies with an M.Sc. in Computer Science, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, achieving Second Class Honours (Upper Division) from the esteemed Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University.

In tandem with my academic endeavors, my professional journey is characterized by a commitment to excellence and innovation. From my pivotal roles as a Reviewer for prestigious conferences and journals to my contributions at esteemed institutions like the National Space Research and Development Agency, I have continually strived to push the boundaries of knowledge in Computer Science. With a particular focus on Cryptography and Network Security Analysis, my expertise extends beyond academia to nurturing the next generation of computer scientists as demonstrated during my tenure at Arewa Model Private School.
